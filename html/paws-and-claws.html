Project Overview

This UI/UX design is tailored for a pet food ordering platform. The design ensures a seamless experience for users to select their pet type, browse a variety of pet foods, and conveniently add items to the cart. The intuitive flow guides users through placing orders with ease. Highlights include pet-specific food recommendations, a clean and visually appealing layout, and a responsive design optimized for multiple devices. The goal is to simplify pet food shopping while enhancing user satisfaction.
