Auto Rent Pro

Standalone Application

2024-2025

Project Overview

Auto Rent Pro is a Vehicle Rental Management System designed to streamline the process of renting vehicles for customers and managing fleet operations for rental businesses. It provides an intuitive and efficient solution for handling reservations, vehicle availability, payments, and customer management.

Key Features

  • User Management: Admins can add, update, and remove users and customers.
  • Vehicle Management: Add, update, and track available vehicles.
  • Reservation System: Users can book and manage rental reservations.
  • Payment Integration: Secure payment processing for rentals & automated invoice generation..
  • Email Notifications: Booking confirmations & reminders.
  • Vehicle Maintenance & Damage Tracking – Log maintenance & repair history.
  • Mileage Tracking – Monitor vehicle usage & fuel efficiency.

Why Auto Rent Pro?

  • Scalable & Maintainable: Layered architecture ensures modularity.
  • Efficient & Secure: Reduces redundancy and improves performance
  • User-Friendly Interface: JavaFX provides a smooth and intuitive experience.
